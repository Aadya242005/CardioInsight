Insight Report — Heart Failure Mortality Prediction

Dataset: Heart Failure Clinical Records (UCI)

Models

Two models were prepared: Logistic Regression and Random Forest. Random Forest generally gives stronger ROC-AUC on this dataset and is used for feature importance.

Top Predictive Features (summary)

  • Age — older age is associated with higher risk of mortality.
  • Serum Creatinine — marker of renal function; higher values predict worse outcomes.
  • Ejection Fraction — lower EF indicates poorer cardiac function.
  • Platelets — may reflect comorbid conditions and inflammation.
  • Serum Sodium — abnormalities correlate with heart failure severity.

Clinical Interpretation

The model highlights well-established clinical risk factors. These features could be used to prioritize patients for more intensive monitoring or early interventions.
